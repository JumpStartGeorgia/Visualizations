Georgian Soldiers Have Died In International Peacekeeping Missions

"Where we protect peace, we protect Georgia."
Tina Khidasheli, Minister of Defense of Georgia 2015
Georgian soldiers have served in a number of peacekeeping and security missions around the world since 1999, including in Kosovo, Africa, Iraq, and Afghanistan. There are currently up to 885 Georgian soldiers serving in Afghanistan.
