Winter 2014-2015 Lari Depreciation Calculator

The Georgian Lari has dramatically depreciated in value against the U.S. Dollar (USD) between November 2014 and February 2015. On 17 November 2014, 1 USD was worth 1.76 Lari, while on 17 February 2015, 1 USD was worth 2.05 Lari. That is a change of 16.48% in just 3 months.
